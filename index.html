Recent Project Overview

Enhancing the International Student Experience

fall 2024

This was my design case study project for the Bits Of Good Design Bootcamp. My partner and I chose to address the issues faced by international students at Georgia Tech by designing an extension to the Office of International Education website. Our goals were to streamline communication between students and the Office Of International Education as well as having organized, relevent information. In addition, allowing students to connect this other international students easily to create a network of support.

view final presentation

We started initailly with conducting primary and secondary research to brainstorm issues faced by international students. We then met with 3 student to ask questions about their expierences and where they felt they had not been given enough assistance.

After interviews, we finally got into designing our application, we realized quickly we had wayyy too many features we were trying to implement that were not feasible. We finally narrowed it down to 3 key frames, a home, information, and forums pages.

We determined it would be the most accessible if it were an extension to the OIE website where it would be easy to find and be more credible as a information source than a third party site. Over 30 components later we had our high fidelity designs done and prototyped.
